Necesitamos URGENTEMENTE su ayuda!

En la noche del dia 14 de Enero, cuando llegábamos a la ciudad de Mendoza (Argentina) para descansar después de 12 horas de viaje, fuimos asaltados a mano armada. Por mucha suerte estamos todos bien, pero perdimos todo lo que nos pertenecía: nuestro auto, instrumentos, discos, camisetas de la banda, amplificadores, ropa, dinero, mochilas y documentos. Tenemos solamente la ropa que usábamos y nos tenemos los unos a los otros.

No sabemos qué hacer todavía pues no nos resta dinero para comer, mucho menos para conseguir nuestros documentos, comprar nuevos instrumentos y continuar la gira. Estamos sin poder salir de Mendoza! Solo para sacar nuestros documentos necesitamos de R$2000. Para llegar a Santiago, Chile, donde sería nuestro próximo show, son por lo menos R$768. Hemos contado hasta ahora con la solidaridad de amigos, músicos y albergues locales y estamos muy felices de recibir tanto cariño en todos los lugares por los cuales pasamos.

Sin nuestros instrumentos, no tenemos ni como trabajar para juntar dinero para cepillos de dientes! Esta vez no lograremos movernos solos y, por eso, NECESITAMOS SU AYUDA!

Friends,

We are in DESPERATE need of your help!

In the night of Jan 14th, as we arrived in the city of Mendoza (Argentina) to rest after 12 hours on the road, we were robbed at gunpoint. It's really lucky that we are all fine, but we lost everything: the car, instruments, the albums, band t-shirts, ampers, clothes, money, backpacks and documents. All we've kept are the clothes we were wearing and each other

We don't know what to do because we don't have money to eat, much less to pay for new documents, new instruments and to continue our tour. We can't even leave Mendoza! The documents alone cost BRL 2,000. To get to Santigo - Chile, where our next show is, we need at least BRL 768. Accommodations, transportation within the city and other incidental costs may amount to another R$1,000 unless we get place to stay for free and nothing else goes wrong.

Up until now, we have relied on the solidarity of friends, musicians and local hostels and we are very glad for the great deal of love we've been given.

Without our instruments, we don't even have a way to work for money to buy toothbrushes! This time we can't move on by ourselves, that's why WE NEED YOUR HELP!

There are many ways to help: with contributions, helping spread the word or simply by sending some love!

Thank you all very much!
The Francisco, el Hombre family
